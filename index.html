Water quality management


This involves protecting and improving the quality of water resources by preventing pollution, treating wastewater, and promoting good water quality practices.


Water conservation


This involves reducing water usage through various means, such as fixing leaks, using water-efficient appliances, and promoting public education campaigns to encourage water conservation.

Water distribution


This involves managing the distribution of water to various users, including households, businesses, and industries. It may involve building and maintaining water supply infrastructure, such as dams, pipelines, and treatment facilities.
